About the app

OptiListen helps you get better at listening. Quickly set goals and track how much time you’re speaking on video and audio calls. Unlock the listener within.

About us

Long Sky Media publishes books, makes apps, and tries to bring a wee bit of trout music to the world. OptiListen was created by Dan Brodnitz and Christian Crumlish, who also fancy themselves its first customers. The app was designed by Elaine Phong and developed by Fractal Labs. The website was designed by Josh Eisenstat. Rebecca Baror provided design direction.

Questions you may have

Q: Why did you all make this app?
A: We were working on our own listening skills, and realized that talk talk was going to break our spirits, so we created a hands-free app that could let us focus less on the buttons and more on trying to be a little more present for the people on our audio and video calls.
Q: Do I need to wear headphones for the app to work?
A: Yes -- that way the app hears that you are talking but not the other folks on the call.
Q: Does the app collect any personal data?
A: It doesn't -- no login, no data. All data is kept on your phone.
Q: Are you all better listeners now?
A: Better, hopefully. But it's a journey! :-) Be hear now, good people!
